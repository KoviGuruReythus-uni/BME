## Argumentation
- It is a _verbal, social, rational_ activity which is aimed at persuading a _reasonable_ critic of the acceptability of a standpoint

### To what extent are debates rational or emotional?
- Naturally, we form standpoints, not just opinions.
	- These standpoints are based on reasons
		- But, not JUST reasons
	- We also _like_ our standpoints, we stand for them and hence we attach emotion to them. 
- For this reason, it is very hard to separate emotional and reason. 

### Product vs Process

| Product                                                       | Process                                                                                                       |
| ------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Using propositions in order to justify another proposition(s) | When people argue, they are usually trying to justify their standpoints in order to persuade the other person |
